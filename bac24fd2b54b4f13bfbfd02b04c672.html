10 products that enhance the male sexual organ

Food has a direct effect on sex life, changing the level of hormones, neurotransmitters and energy. Some products improve the blood supply to the genitals, others give energy, improve mood or stimulate the synthesis of sex hormones.

To strengthen the male sexual organ, you just need to add some of the following products to your diet.

1. Figs.

Figs were a favorite delicacy in Ancient Greece and Rome, probably because of their ability to ignite passion. On it is rich in magnesium, which is necessary for the synthesis of sex hormones.

Figs stimulates the secretion of pheromones and increases fertility. You can see this for yourself by eating five figs.

2. Watermelon.

Scientists have found out that watermelon contains a substance called citrulline, which has a similar effect to the assistant A. In the body, citrulline is converted to L-arginine, an amino acid that increases nitric oxide levels and causes blood vessels to dilate. As a result, the blood supply to the genitals improves.

Low nitric oxide levels are associated with mental and physical fatigue, as well as sexual fatigue dysfunction.

3. Chocolate.

Chocolate is a great aphrodisiac, and it also contains magnesium, which helps you relax. Dark chocolate has been shown to include the substance phenylethylamine, which causes the release of the same endorphins that are released during sex.

4. Bananas.

Bananas - a very good aphrodisiac, they contain the enzyme bromelain, which stimulates the production of male hormone. In addition, bananas are rich in potassium and vitamin B, which provide energy.

5. The chili pepper.

Hot pepper literally makes a person hot. The capsaicin contained in it accelerates the heartbeat, improves blood circulation, increases sweating-signs that are usually observed during sexual arousal.

6. Oysters.

Oysters are a well-known aphrodisiac. They are rich in zinc, which improves the blood supply to the genitals. It was found that oysters contain amino acids, stimulating the production of estrogen and male hormone, and increasing sex hormones increases sexual desire.

7. Eggs.

Eggs are rich in protein and fill the body with energy. In addition, they are a good source of the amino acid L-arginine, which helps in the treatment of CVD and erectile dysfunction. Yolk provides vitamins B6 and B12, which are necessary for the production of serotonin - a neurotransmitter responsible for good mood.

8. Med.

Honey is a good source of boron, a mineral that helps in the metabolism of estrogen. It has been found that this mineral also raises the level of male hormone in the body.

9. Blueberries.

Blueberries, strawberries and raspberries are rich in vitamins C and E, which increase skin sensitivity and increase sexual desire. These delicious berries contain many phytochemicals that increase sexual stamina. Eat 10 blueberries in a few days hours before making love.

10. Pumpkin seeds.

Pumpkin seeds are known for their reputation as a natural aphrodisiac. They contain a lot of zinc, which is necessary for the synthesis of male hormone. In addition, pumpkin seeds have been shown to reduce the risk of prostate cancer.
